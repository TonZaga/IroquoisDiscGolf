DIRECTIONS TO COURSE - North bound

From Interstate 65 North:

    Take exit 125B to merge onto KY-841 W/Gene Snyder Fwy
    Take exit 6 for New Cut Rd
    Keep right at the fork, follow signs for New Cut Rd North and merge onto New Cut Rd
    Merge onto New Cut Rd
    Turn left onto Frisbee Way
    Hole 1 runs parallel with parking lot

DIRECTIONS TO COURSE - South bound

From Interstate 65 South:

    Take exit 132 for Crittenden Dr toward Fair/ Expo Center Gates 2-3-4
    Turn right onto Crittenden Dr
    Turn right onto Central Ave
    Turn left onto S 3rd St
    Continue onto Southern Pkwy
    Continue straight to stay on Southern Pkwy
    Turn left onto New Cut Rd
    Turn right onto Frisbee Way
    Hole 1 runs parallel with parking lot
