Disc Golf News

Congrats to Kevin Jones!!

It was a great week of DGPT Championship action that saw the field of 16 players, down to 1 man! Kevin Jones held off the likes of Chris Dickerson, Austin Hannum, and Calvin Heimburg. Chris Dickerson scored a bogey on the final hole to allow Kevin to avoid a playoff and seal the victory, with just a PAR. Kevin finished with a -9 (55) final round and earned the $20,000 payday!
